Privacy Policy for aiPostPics

Effective Date: December 18, 2023

aiPostPics ("we," "our," "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting the information you share with us. This Privacy Policy explains how our app collects, uses, and shares your information when you use our service to connect Instagram Business/Creator accounts, Facebook Pages, and Dropbox folders for automated posting.

By using our app, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

1. Information We Collect

When you use aiPostPics, we collect the following information:

a) Facebook and Instagram Account Data

When you connect your Instagram Business/Creator account and Facebook Page using our app, we collect:

  • Your Facebook Page ID and Page Name
  • Your Instagram Account ID and Account Name
  • Access tokens required to interact with the Instagram Graph API and Facebook Graph API

These permissions are used to enable content posting and validation.

b) Dropbox Data

  • We collect access to a specified Dropbox folder that you choose.
  • Images and media files from the folder are accessed to facilitate posting.

c) Generated Content

  • Our app processes images using AI to generate captions and hashtags.
  • This content is stored temporarily to complete the posting process.

d) Log and Usage Data

We may collect basic log information, including API call responses, errors, and timestamps, for troubleshooting and analytics.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information solely to provide and improve the functionality of the aiPostPics app:

  • To connect to Instagram Business/Creator accounts and Facebook Pages via the Instagram Graph API.
  • To access images from your Dropbox folder and post them to your connected platforms.
  • To generate captions and hashtags using AI for enhanced content.
  • To schedule and automate the posting of images as configured by you.
  • To validate that your Instagram and Facebook connections are correctly established.

We do not share, sell, or use your information for any purposes outside of the scope of this app.

3. Sharing Your Information

We share your information only in the following circumstances:

  • Third-Party APIs: We interact with third-party APIs (Dropbox, Facebook Graph API, and Instagram Graph API) to enable core app functionality.
  • Legal Requirements: If required by law or to protect our rights, we may disclose necessary information.

4. Data Retention and Storage

We retain the collected information only as long as necessary to provide our services:

  • Temporary media and generated captions are deleted after the post is completed.
  • Tokens and account IDs are stored securely to maintain platform connections and are removed upon user request.
  • All data is stored on secure servers located in the European Union, in compliance with GDPR regulations.
  • We do not transfer your data outside the European Economic Area (EEA) unless necessary for the operation of third-party services (Facebook, Instagram, Dropbox) that you explicitly connect to our service.

5. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure. Your access tokens and credentials are encrypted and stored securely. However, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

6. Your Choices and Data Deletion

You have the following options regarding your data:

  • Revoke Access: You can disconnect your Facebook Page, Instagram account, or Dropbox folder at any time through your app settings.
  • Data Deletion Process:
    1. Submit a deletion request to jj@jonnyjelinek.com with your account details
    2. We will process your request within 30 days
    3. You will receive confirmation once your data has been deleted
    4. If you're not satisfied with our response, you can appeal by contacting us again with "DELETION APPEAL" in the subject line
  • Account Deletion: You may request complete account deletion, which will remove all your data from our systems

7. Third-Party Services

Our app interacts with third-party platforms (Facebook, Instagram, Dropbox) to provide its core functionality. Please refer to their respective privacy policies for more information:

  • Facebook Privacy Policy
  • Instagram Privacy Policy
  • Dropbox Privacy Policy

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our app or legal requirements. Any updates will be posted on this page, and the effective date will be updated accordingly.
